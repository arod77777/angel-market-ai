HOW TO USE
THE DASHBOARD

5 steps to start trading smarter. Read once, trade better every day.

1
OPEN THE DASHBOARD

Click Launch Dashboard or Start Free Trial on the main page. The app opens full screen over the landing page. Crypto loads instantly. Stocks take about 15 seconds to pull from Yahoo Finance.

Hit ↻ Refresh anytime for the latest data. The dashboard auto-refreshes every 3 minutes on its own. Use the Crypto and Stocks tabs at the top to switch markets.

2
READ THE TABLE

Each row is one asset, sorted by Edge Score highest first. Here is what every column means:

AI Score (1–10)
7.5+ = Buy. 4.5–7.5 = Hold. Below 4.5 = Sell. Based on momentum, volume, and market data. Recalculates every refresh.
Signal
▲ BUY = strong upward momentum. ◆ HOLD = wait and watch. ▼ SELL = exit zone or downward pressure.
RSI
Below 30 = Oversold — bounce is likely. Above 70 = Overbought — reversal risk. Most traders miss oversold entries.
Whale Signal
Accumulating = large wallets buying quietly before price moves. Usually 24–72 hours early. Your earliest possible signal.
Catalyst
Upcoming event in 7 days: earnings, Fed meeting, ETF inclusion, insider buy. Get in before the event, not after.
Edge Score
Combines AI Score, RSI, Whale, Volume, and Catalyst. Table sorts by this. Highest = best opportunity right now.
3
USE THE FILTERS

The filter bar cuts noise instantly. Most useful combinations:

🟢
Buy
Assets with AI Score above 7.5. Your highest-conviction setups right now.
⚡
Alerts — check this first every session
Assets where 2+ signals fired simultaneously. This is your jump signal. Review these before anything else.
🐳
Whale
Assets with active bullish whale signals. Large wallets moving before price does.
↓
Top Losers
Losers with Oversold RSI are often the best bounce setups. Everyone sold already. You buy the dip before recovery.
📊
Stock category filters (Stocks tab only)
S&P 500, Nasdaq, Dividend, Low Risk, Penny. Switch to Stocks tab to see these filters appear.
4
GET THE AI TRADING BRIEF

Click ⚡ Analyze on any row. Claude AI reads every signal and returns a structured 4-part brief in seconds:

SITUATION
What all signals say right now, in 2 clear sentences. Instant setup understanding without reading a chart.
EDGE
Why this might move before the crowd. Names specific signals. This is the most important section.
RISK
The single biggest threat to this trade. Read before acting. If you cannot accept this risk, do not enter.
ACTION
Entry zone, confirmation to wait for, stop loss, position sizing. Specific numbers, not vague advice.
5
ALERTS AND SOUND

Alerts fire automatically when multiple signals align on one asset. You do not need to watch the screen constantly.

Purple alert bar — appears at the top showing which asset fired and exactly which signals triggered it.
Purple badge on the row — a ⚡ appears next to the asset name so you spot it instantly while scanning.
Sound toggle — off by default. Click 🔇 Sound Off in the top bar to enable audio pings when alerts fire. Works great when the app is in a background tab.
Best daily workflow: Open app → click Alerts filter → check Edge Score column → click ⚡ Analyze on top pick → read ACTION → decide.
